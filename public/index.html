About TED

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages.

TED stands for Technology, Entertainment, Design — three broad subject areas that are collectively shaping our world. But a TED conference is broader still, showcasing important research and ideas from all disciplines and exploring how they connect.

TED is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. TED's success is in bringing together the world's most remarkable people across many fields. The result? Unexpected connections. Extraordinary insights. Powerful inspiration.

About TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx brings the spirit of TED to local communities around the globe through TEDx events. These events are organized by passionate individuals who seek to uncover new ideas and to share the latest research in their local areas that spark conversations in their communities. TEDx events include live speakers and recorded TED Talks, and are organized independently under a free license granted by TED. These events are not controlled by TED, but event organizers agree to abide by our format, and are offered guidelines for curation, speaker coaching, event organizing and more. They learn from us and from each other. More than 3000 events are now held annually.

About the event

Hello! We are Luna and Luisa and we are the main organizers of TEDxJacobsUniversity, an independent TEDx event happening on March 26, 2022 in Bremen.

TED is an annual event that brings together the world's leading thinkers and doers to share ideas that matter in every discipline — technology, entertainment, design, science, humanities, business, and development. The talks at the conference, called TED Talks, are then made available to watch for free on TED.com.

In the spirit of "ideas worth spreading," TED has created TEDx, a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connections.

We are excited to be a TEDx licensee. As students at the most international university in Germany, we are happy to involve our international community in such an inspiring event. We believe that holding a TEDxJacobsUniversity will be a great way to promote the flow of ideas, build connections and broaden the horizons! This year, our theme is “Incrementum: Growth in Collaboration”. We hope you follow and join us on this amazing adventure!

- Luna and Luisa, TEDxJacobsUniversity Main Organizers

Lookback at TEDx2018

TEDxJacobsUniversity is not a new occurrence on the Jacobs University campus. In 2018, a group of students set up a TEDx event based around the theme of a strong, fresh wind spreading collections of stories and ideas. Similar to the mass spreading of the seeds of a dandelion, the ideas impact and inspire their societies. In the true spirit of ‘Ideas Worth Spreading’, the participants of TEDxJacobsUniversity 2018 had the opportunity to broaden their horizon and enrich their knowledge on the diverse disciplines of science, education, music, gastronomy, etc. while exploring topics of innovation, communication and collaboration.
