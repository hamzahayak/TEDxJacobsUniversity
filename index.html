About the event

Hello! We are Luna and Luisa and we are the main organizers of TEDxJacobsUniversity, an independent TEDx event happening on March 26, 2022 in Bremen.

TED is an annual event that brings together the world's leading thinkers and doers to share ideas that matter in every discipline — technology, entertainment, design, science, humanities, business, and development. The talks at the conference, called TED Talks, are then made available to watch for free on TED.com.

We are excited to be a TEDx licensee. As students at the most international university in Germany, we are happy to involve our international community in such an inspiring event. We believe that holding a TEDxJacobsUniversity will be a great way to promote the flow of ideas, build connections and broaden the horizons! This year, our theme is “Incrementum: Growth in Collaboration”.

We hope you follow and join us on this amazing adventure!

Luna and Luisa

About TEDx


In the spirit of "ideas worth spreading," TED has created TEDx, a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connections.
